Step1 - On your computer, create and edit an .htm file (i.e. 'hello.htm').

Step2 - Copy the above code to the clipBoard then paste clipBoard contents to your .htm file. IMPORTANT:  In your .htm file, change the nickname: 'xxxx', member : 'xxxx@xxxx.com', and pin: '1234' to valid values. Nickname is any unique one word name other members will know you by. Member is any valid email address you use. Pin is 4 or more digits that you can remember for future logins. (Example >> nickname: 'BillyBob', member: 'jimmy@gmail.com', pin: '92109').

Step3 - Once your changes are made, save your .htm file to any convenient place on your computer. Now open your .htm file in your web Browser (I use Chrome by right clicking on the .htm file and selecting open). If all goes well, you should get the results of running your minion displayed on your browser!

Troubles? Double check the changes you made for syntax or spelling issues. Look at your browser's console looking for any errors logged there. Or talk to us at our forum, so we can personally help out. https://minionlogic.freeforums.net/
