Are you tired of coding for others on team projects that aren't challenging, satisfying, or make money? Or maybe you're a hobbyist who loves to use their imagination to create new and exiting things? Then consider becoming an independent distributed web component provider! Here we simply call those Minions and their providers Minion Makers.

A Minion Maker designs, develops, their own Minions to do anything they want on there own equipment and language of choice to produce Minions that are useful to the Clients that will use them.

Minions are easy to make and you serve them from your equipment full-time via standardized web sockets protocols and JSON serialized objects. When a Client orders your Minion, you will receive a JSON order for you to fill. Once filled, you return the Minion JSON results back to the Client. When the Client receives your Minions work, minionLogic will bill and collect usage fees from the Client

The best part, is that for every billable client that uses your Minion, you will be able to receive a royalty from the Clients that use your Minion! You can elect to receive one of three royalty rates. You can elect a usage royalty rate, an exclusive usage rate, or for a limited time, a founders royalty rate.
